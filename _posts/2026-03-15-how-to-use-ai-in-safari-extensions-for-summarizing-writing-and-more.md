## AI Belongs in Your Browser

AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini have changed how people work with information. But using them typically means switching to a separate app or website, copying content, pasting it into the AI tool, waiting for a response, and then switching back to your original context. This constant context-switching breaks your flow and adds unnecessary friction.

Safari extensions bring AI capabilities directly into your browsing experience. Summarize an article without leaving the page. Get writing assistance inline. Generate prompts that get better results from AI services. All of it happens right where you need it — inside Safari.

## Extension AI: Your In-Browser AI Assistant

[Extension AI](/apps/extension-ai/) is a Safari extension that integrates AI capabilities directly into your browsing workflow. Instead of copying text to a separate AI chat, you work with AI right on the web page you are reading.

### Summarize Articles and Long Content

The most immediately useful AI feature for browsing is summarization. When you land on a long article, research paper, or documentation page, Extension AI can generate a concise summary so you can quickly determine whether the content is worth reading in full.

#### How to Summarize a Page

1. **Install Extension AI** from the App Store and enable it in Safari.
2. **Navigate to the article** or page you want to summarize.
3. **Click the Extension AI icon** in the toolbar.
4. **Select Summarize.** The extension processes the page content and generates a concise summary.
5. **Read the summary** to decide if you want to read the full article.

This is especially powerful for:

- **Research.** Quickly triage dozens of search results to find the most relevant sources.
- **News consumption.** Get the key points from news articles without reading thousands of words.
- **Documentation.** Understand the purpose of a documentation page before diving into the details.
- **Email newsletters.** Summarize long newsletter emails to extract the actionable items.

### Generate and Edit Text

Extension AI is not just for reading — it also helps with writing. Whether you are composing an email, drafting a comment, filling out a form, or writing a social media post, the extension can assist directly on the page.

#### Writing Capabilities

- **Text generation** from a brief prompt or outline.
- **Rewriting** to improve clarity, tone, or conciseness.
- **Tone adjustment** to make text more formal, casual, persuasive, or friendly.
- **Grammar and spelling correction** with explanations of changes.
- **Expansion** of brief notes into full paragraphs.
- **Translation** using AI to translate text with more natural phrasing than traditional translation engines. For dedicated translation, see our guide on [translating web pages in Safari](/blog/safari-extensions/how-to-translate-web-pages-in-safari-on-iphone-and-mac/).

### Ask Questions About Page Content

Highlight a passage on any web page and ask Extension AI a question about it. The extension uses the page content as context to provide relevant, accurate answers.

- "What are the key arguments in this article?"
- "Explain this technical concept in simpler terms."
- "What are the counterarguments to this position?"
- "How does this compare to [another topic]?"

This turns passive reading into active learning, helping you engage more deeply with the content you consume.

## Prompt Wizard: Craft Better AI Prompts

Getting good results from AI tools depends heavily on how you phrase your prompts. Vague or poorly structured prompts lead to generic, unhelpful responses. [Prompt Wizard](/apps/prompt-wizard/) helps you craft more effective prompts for any AI service.

### How Prompt Wizard Works

1. **Install Prompt Wizard** from the App Store and enable it in Safari.
2. **Open any AI chat interface** — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or others — in Safari.
3. **Click the Prompt Wizard icon** before typing your prompt.
4. **Describe what you want** in plain language.
5. **Prompt Wizard generates an optimized prompt** that is more likely to produce the results you need.
6. **Use the generated prompt** in your AI conversation.

### Why Better Prompts Matter

The difference between a mediocre AI response and an excellent one often comes down to prompt engineering. Prompt Wizard applies best practices automatically:

- **Specificity:** Adds relevant context and constraints to your request.
- **Role assignment:** Frames the AI as an expert in the relevant domain.
- **Output formatting:** Specifies the desired format (bullet points, step-by-step, comparison table, etc.).
- **Examples:** Includes few-shot examples when they would improve the response quality.
- **Guardrails:** Adds instructions that reduce hallucination and improve accuracy.

### Use Cases for Prompt Wizard

- **Content creation.** Generate detailed, well-structured prompts for blog posts, emails, reports, and other written content.
- **Coding assistance.** Craft prompts that produce accurate, well-commented code with proper error handling.
- **Research questions.** Frame research queries to get comprehensive, well-sourced answers.
- **Creative projects.** Generate prompts for creative writing, brainstorming, or design ideation.
- **Learning.** Create prompts that produce clear, pedagogically sound explanations of complex topics.

## Using Extension AI and Prompt Wizard Together

These two extensions complement each other in a powerful workflow:

1. **Use Prompt Wizard** to craft an optimized prompt for your AI task.
2. **Use Extension AI** to execute the task directly on the web page you are reading, without switching to a separate AI chat.

For tasks that require a dedicated AI conversation (complex projects, multi-turn dialogues), use Prompt Wizard to optimize your prompts before entering them in ChatGPT, Claude, or your preferred AI service.

## Privacy and AI Extensions

When using AI extensions, be mindful of what data you are sending to AI services:

- **Avoid processing sensitive information** like passwords, financial data, or confidential business documents through AI extensions.
- **Check the extension's privacy policy** to understand what data is transmitted and how it is handled.
- **Use local processing options** when available for sensitive content.
