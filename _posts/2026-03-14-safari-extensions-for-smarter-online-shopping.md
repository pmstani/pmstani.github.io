## Shop Smarter, Not Harder

Online shopping has become second nature, but are you getting the best deal? Are you really understanding what you are buying, especially when shopping on international websites or purchasing food products? Safari extensions can give you the information you need to make better purchasing decisions, right alongside the product pages you are browsing.

Two Safari extensions stand out for making online shopping more informed: one handles currency and unit conversions for international shopping, and the other analyzes nutritional information for healthier food choices.

## Convert: Currency and Unit Conversions in Safari

[Convert](/apps/convert/) is a Safari extension that handles currency conversions, unit calculations, and measurement translations directly in your browser. When you are shopping on an international website and prices are in an unfamiliar currency, or a product listing uses metric measurements and you think in imperial (or vice versa), Convert provides instant answers without switching to a calculator app.

{% include blog-cta.html app_slug="convert" %}

### How to Use Convert for Shopping

1. **Install Convert** from the App Store and enable it in Safari's extension settings.
2. **Browse a product page** with prices in a foreign currency or measurements in unfamiliar units.
3. **Select the value** you want to convert, or click the Convert icon in the toolbar.
4. **View the conversion** in your preferred currency or unit system.

### Currency Conversion for International Shopping

Shopping on international websites — whether you are buying electronics from Japan, fashion from Europe, or handcrafted goods from smaller markets — means dealing with foreign currencies. Convert keeps you informed:

- **Real-time exchange rates** so you know the actual cost in your home currency.
- **Multiple currency support** covering all major world currencies and many minor ones.
- **Automatic detection** that recognizes currency symbols and amounts on the page.
- **Historical rates** that help you understand whether the current exchange rate is favorable.

### Unit Conversions for Product Specifications

Product specifications often use measurement systems that differ from what you are accustomed to:

- **Weight:** kilograms to pounds, grams to ounces
- **Length:** centimeters to inches, meters to feet
- **Volume:** liters to gallons, milliliters to fluid ounces
- **Temperature:** Celsius to Fahrenheit
- **Clothing sizes:** EU to US, UK to US, and other regional sizing systems

Convert handles all of these, eliminating the guesswork that leads to purchasing errors, especially for clothing and shoes where sizing differences between countries can be significant.

### Saving Money with Convert

Beyond basic conversion, Convert helps you compare prices across international stores accurately. A product might appear cheaper on a foreign website, but once you factor in the exchange rate, shipping costs, and import duties, the savings might evaporate. Having accurate currency conversion right on the page helps you make true apples-to-apples comparisons.

## Food Scanner for Safari: Nutritional Intelligence

[Food Scanner for Safari](/apps/food-scanner/) brings nutritional analysis to your online grocery and food shopping experience. When browsing food product pages, this extension analyzes the nutritional information and helps you make healthier choices.

### How Food Scanner Works

1. **Install Food Scanner for Safari** from the App Store and enable it.
2. **Browse a food product page** on an online grocery store or food retailer website.
3. **Activate the extension** to analyze the product's nutritional information.
4. **View the analysis**, which breaks down the nutritional content into easy-to-understand ratings and comparisons.

### What Food Scanner Analyzes

- **Macronutrients:** Calories, protein, carbohydrates, fats, and fiber content with visual indicators showing whether levels are high, moderate, or low.
- **Sugar content:** Identifies high-sugar products and compares them to recommended daily intake levels.
- **Sodium levels:** Flags products with excessive sodium content, important for those monitoring salt intake.
- **Ingredient quality:** Highlights artificial additives, preservatives, and other ingredients you might want to avoid.
- **Dietary compatibility:** Helps identify whether products fit specific dietary requirements like low-carb, high-protein, gluten-free, or vegan.

### Making Healthier Choices

Food Scanner transforms online grocery shopping from a guessing game into an informed decision-making process. Instead of squinting at tiny nutrition labels or trying to mentally calculate whether 23 grams of sugar per serving is a lot, the extension gives you clear, actionable information.

This is particularly valuable when:

- **Comparing similar products.** Two brands of granola might look similar, but one could have twice the sugar. Food Scanner highlights these differences.
- **Shopping for dietary needs.** Whether you are managing diabetes, following a heart-healthy diet, or tracking macros for fitness, nutritional analysis on every product page keeps you on track.
- **Discovering hidden ingredients.** Many processed foods contain surprising amounts of sugar, sodium, or artificial ingredients that are easy to miss without careful label reading.
- **Teaching healthy habits.** Seeing nutritional analysis alongside products helps build nutritional literacy over time.

## Combining Both Extensions for International Food Shopping

If you shop for specialty food products from international websites, Convert and Food Scanner work well together. Convert handles the currency and weight conversions (is 500g a good price compared to the 16oz package at your local store?), while Food Scanner analyzes whether the product fits your nutritional goals.
