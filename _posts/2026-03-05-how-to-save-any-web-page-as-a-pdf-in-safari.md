## Why Save Web Pages as PDFs?

Web pages are ephemeral. Articles get updated, pages get redesigned, and entire websites disappear. When you find content worth keeping, saving it as a PDF creates a permanent, portable copy that preserves the original layout, text, and images in a single file.

PDFs are also the standard format for sharing documents professionally. Saving a web receipt as a PDF for expense reporting, capturing a product specification page for reference, or archiving a news article for research are all everyday tasks that become simple with the right tool.

## Using Save as PDF for Safari

Safari has a built-in Print to PDF option, but it often produces awkward results with broken layouts, cut-off content, and unnecessary headers and footers. [Save as PDF](/apps/save-as-pdf/) is a Safari extension designed specifically for creating clean, readable PDF captures of web pages.

## How to Save a Web Page as a PDF

### On Mac

1. **Install Save as PDF.** Open Safari, go to **Safari > Settings > Extensions**, click **More Extensions**, and search for Save as PDF. Install and enable it.
2. **Navigate to the page** you want to save.
3. **Click the Save as PDF icon** in the Safari toolbar.
4. **Configure options** if desired. You can adjust page size, orientation, margins, and whether to include backgrounds and images.
5. **Choose a save location** and file name.
6. **Click Save.** Your PDF is ready.

### On iPhone and iPad

1. **Install and enable** Save as PDF from Settings > Apps > Safari > Extensions.
2. **Open the web page** you want to save.
3. **Tap the extensions menu** and select Save as PDF.
4. **Adjust settings** as needed.
5. **Save the PDF** to your Files app, iCloud Drive, or share it directly.

## When PDF Saving Is Most Useful

### Archiving Articles and Research

Researchers and students often need to save articles for later reference. PDFs maintain formatting and are easy to annotate with tools like Preview on Mac or Markup on iOS. Unlike bookmarks, PDFs do not break when the original page changes or goes offline.

### Saving Receipts and Confirmations

Online purchase confirmations, flight bookings, event tickets, and subscription receipts are all worth saving as PDFs. They serve as proof of purchase and are easy to attach to expense reports.

### Creating Offline Reading Material

While you could use [Website Downloader](/apps/website-downloader/) to save entire sites, sometimes you just want a single article saved for offline reading on a flight or commute. PDFs are lightweight and work in any PDF reader without needing special software. For more extensive offline archiving, see our guide on [downloading entire websites for offline reading](/blog/safari-extensions/how-to-download-an-entire-website-for-offline-reading/).

### Sharing Web Content Professionally

When you need to share web content with colleagues who might not have access to the original page (behind a login, paywall, or on an internal network), a PDF is the cleanest way to share it.

## Tips for Better PDF Captures

**Use reader mode first.** If Safari's Reader Mode is available for a page (the reader icon appears in the address bar), activate it before saving the PDF. Reader mode strips away ads, navigation menus, and other clutter, producing a much cleaner PDF.

**Adjust margins.** If the default margins are too wide or narrow for your needs, customize them in the extension settings before saving.

**Check the page length.** Some web pages are extremely long with lazy-loaded content. Scroll through the entire page before saving to ensure all content is loaded.

**Organize your PDFs.** Create a folder structure for your saved PDFs — by topic, date, or project — so you can find them later. A folder full of unsorted PDFs is almost as unhelpful as not saving them at all.

## Save as PDF vs. Other Approaches

**Safari Print to PDF:** Built-in but produces lower-quality results with layout issues and page breaks in awkward places.

**Screenshot stitching:** Taking multiple screenshots and combining them is slow and produces image files rather than searchable text.

**Save as PDF extension:** Purpose-built for clean captures with configurable output. The best option for most users.

**Complete website download:** For saving multiple pages or an entire site structure, [Website Downloader](/apps/website-downloader/) or [Site Slurpr](/apps/site-slurpr/) are better suited. Save as PDF excels at capturing individual pages.
