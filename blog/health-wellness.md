Your iPhone can be more than a distraction — it can be a genuine tool for better mental and physical health. From AI-powered sound therapy for tinnitus to guided meditation, mood tracking, and even surf forecasting, the right apps help you build healthier daily habits.

This guide covers wellness apps that are private, lightweight, and focused on real results.

## Mental Health & Mood Tracking
Understanding your emotional patterns is the first step to improving them. Modern mood tracking apps make it simple to log how you feel and spot trends over time.

## Meditation & Mindfulness
You do not need a 30-minute session to benefit from meditation. Micro-meditation apps offer 1-3 minute exercises that fit into any schedule — perfect for a calm reset between meetings or tasks.

## Sound Therapy & Tinnitus Relief
Millions of people live with tinnitus. AI-powered sound therapy apps create personalized audio environments based on recent research to help mask and reduce symptoms.

## Positive Affirmations & Motivation
Daily affirmations backed by research in positive psychology can shift mindset over time. The right app delivers personalized affirmations without clutter or subscriptions.

## Physical Health & Fitness
Export your Apple Health data for deeper analysis, track outdoor activities like surfing with accurate forecasts, and build healthy habits for the whole family.
