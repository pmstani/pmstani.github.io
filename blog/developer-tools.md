Building and marketing apps requires the right toolkit. From tracking your App Store keyword rankings to inspecting competitor websites, analyzing SEO, and staying current with tech news, these tools give developers a real edge.

This guide covers developer-focused apps and Safari extensions that solve real workflow problems.

## App Store Optimization (ASO)
Track keyword rankings with hourly updates, monitor multiple geographies, and understand how your apps perform in search — all from your home screen.

## Web Inspection & Debugging
Inspect HTML elements, debug CSS, format JSON responses, and analyze website technology stacks directly in Safari on iPhone and Mac.

## SEO Analysis
Audit on-page SEO factors, extract links for analysis, and understand how search engines see your content.

## Content & Documentation
Convert HTML to Markdown for documentation, manage RSS feeds for research, and stay on top of industry news without doomscrolling social media.

## Tech News & Community
Curated tech news from Hacker News and other sources, RSS reader support, and focused reading experiences that respect your time.
