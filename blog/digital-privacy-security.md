Your digital life deserves protection. From passwords to photos to insurance inventories, the right apps help you stay secure, organized, and in control of your data — without sending everything to the cloud.

This guide covers privacy-first apps that keep your information local and secure.

## Password Management
Strong, unique passwords for every account are essential. KeePass-compatible apps let you manage credentials locally without trusting a third-party cloud service.

## Storage & Photo Cleanup
Duplicate photos and forgotten screenshots silently eat your storage. Smart cleanup tools identify and remove duplicates so you can reclaim gigabytes.

## Document Management
Compress oversized PDFs, organize files, and keep important documents accessible without cluttering your device.

## Asset & Inventory Tracking
Home inventory apps help you document everything you own — essential for insurance claims. Equipment tracking apps keep maintenance schedules and asset records organized for small businesses.

## Privacy-Respecting Utilities
Weather apps, calculators, and other daily-use tools that work without tracking you or selling your data.
