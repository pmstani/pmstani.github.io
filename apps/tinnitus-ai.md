## About Tinnitus AI

Tinnitus AI is a dedicated sound therapy application that leverages artificial intelligence to deliver personalized relief for tinnitus sufferers. If you experience persistent ringing, buzzing, humming, or other phantom sounds, this app creates tailored soundscapes designed to mask and gradually reduce your symptoms over time.

Unlike generic white noise apps, Tinnitus AI adapts to your specific hearing profile. The AI engine analyzes your tinnitus frequency and intensity, then generates custom audio therapies that target your unique symptoms. Over time, the app learns from your feedback to refine its approach, providing increasingly effective relief sessions.

With background audio support, you can use Tinnitus AI throughout your day — while working, studying, or falling asleep. Track your tinnitus patterns over time to identify triggers and measure improvement, giving you greater control over your hearing health.
