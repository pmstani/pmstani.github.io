## About Ultra Wide for Insta

Ultra Wide for Insta lets you shoot cinematic ultra-wide aspect ratio videos optimized for Instagram and other social media platforms directly from your iPhone. Create professional-looking content with letterbox effects and widescreen formats that give your videos a distinctive, film-like quality.

Standard phone video looks like standard phone video. Ultra Wide for Insta transforms your recordings with cinematic aspect ratios that immediately elevate the production value of your content. Multiple preset ratios — from subtle widescreen to dramatic ultra-wide letterbox — let you choose the exact look you want.

The app is designed for direct sharing to Instagram and other social platforms. Record, preview your cinematic footage, and share it to your audience without needing separate video editing software. The result is professional-looking social media content created entirely on your iPhone.

- Content creators wanting a cinematic look for their Instagram videos

## Why choose Ultra Wide for Insta?

Ultra Wide for Insta provides the cinematic look that normally requires expensive equipment or complex editing software. The preset aspect ratios and letterbox effects are applied in real-time during recording, eliminating post-production work.

Direct social media sharing integration means you can go from recording to posting in seconds. It is the fastest way to create cinematic-quality video content on iPhone.
