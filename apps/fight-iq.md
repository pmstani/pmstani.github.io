## About Fight IQ

Fight IQ is an AI-powered training companion designed for combat sports enthusiasts. Whether you practice boxing, MMA, kickboxing, or traditional martial arts, this app delivers personalized training programs, a comprehensive technique library, and fight knowledge quizzes to help you elevate your skills.

The AI engine generates training plans tailored to your experience level, goals, and available equipment. Instead of following generic workout routines, you get programs that adapt to your specific discipline and areas for improvement. The technique library covers strikes, defenses, combinations, and strategies across multiple combat sports.

Beyond physical training, Fight IQ tests and expands your fight knowledge with quizzes covering rules, strategy, and the history of combat sports. Understanding the mental side of fighting is just as important as physical conditioning, and this app helps you develop both.

## Who is Fight IQ for?

- MMA practitioners looking for structured, AI-generated training plans
- Boxers wanting to expand their technique knowledge and workout variety
- Martial arts students preparing for belt tests or competitions
- Combat sports fans who want to deepen their fight knowledge
- Beginners seeking guided entry into combat sports training on iPhone

## Why choose Fight IQ?

Fight IQ combines AI-personalized training with a deep technique library, giving you both the workout plan and the knowledge to execute it properly. This dual approach accelerates learning and helps prevent the plateau that comes from repetitive training.

The app adapts to your journey. As you improve, your training plans evolve. Fight IQ quizzes keep your knowledge growing alongside your physical skills, creating a well-rounded combat sports education on iOS.
