## About PDF Compressor - ThinPDF

PDF Compressor - ThinPDF reduces the file size of your PDF documents while maintaining visual quality on macOS. When PDFs are too large to email, upload to websites, or store efficiently, ThinPDF compresses them quickly with multiple compression levels to match your needs.

Large PDF files are a common frustration. Email attachment limits, slow uploads, and wasted storage space all stem from oversized documents. ThinPDF solves this by offering intelligent compression that significantly reduces file sizes — often by 50% or more — while keeping text sharp and images clear enough for their intended use.

Multiple compression levels let you choose the right balance between file size and quality. Preview the compressed result before saving to ensure it meets your standards. Batch processing support means you can compress multiple PDFs at once, saving even more time for large document collections.

## Who is PDF Compressor - ThinPDF for?

- Professionals who need to email large PDF documents
- Anyone uploading PDFs to websites with file size limits
- Students sharing assignments and research papers
- Businesses looking to reduce document storage costs
- Mac users who want to compress multiple PDFs in batch

## Why choose PDF Compressor - ThinPDF?

ThinPDF provides the precise control over compression that generic tools lack. Multiple compression levels let you optimize for your specific use case — maximum compression for email attachments or lighter compression for print-quality documents.

The preview feature ensures you never accidentally degrade quality below your standards. Combined with batch processing, it is the most practical and efficient PDF compression tool available on macOS.
