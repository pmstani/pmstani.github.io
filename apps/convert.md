## About Convert

Convert is a Safari extension for macOS that automatically detects and converts currencies on web pages to your preferred currency. Shop internationally with confidence by seeing prices in your local currency as you browse — no manual conversion needed.

International online shopping often means mentally converting prices from unfamiliar currencies, which leads to confusion and purchasing mistakes. Convert eliminates this by detecting prices on web pages and displaying the equivalent in your local currency in real time, using current exchange rates.

With support for over 150 currencies and real-time exchange rate updates, you always see accurate, current conversions. The inline conversion displays prices directly on the page where you see them, creating a seamless shopping experience that feels like every store lists prices in your currency.

## Who is Convert for?

- International online shoppers comparing prices across different countries
- Travelers researching costs in foreign currencies
- Business professionals reviewing international pricing and invoices
- Expats and immigrants who think in one currency but shop in another
- Anyone who encounters foreign currencies while browsing on Mac

## Why choose Convert?

Convert works automatically — it detects currencies without any action from you and displays conversions inline on the page. This passive approach means you never need to copy prices into a separate converter tool.

Real-time rates and support for 150+ currencies ensure accuracy and coverage for virtually any shopping or browsing scenario. It is the most convenient currency conversion tool for Safari on macOS.
