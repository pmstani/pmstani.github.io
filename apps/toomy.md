## About Toomy

Toomy makes tooth brushing fun for kids and adults by playing music for the recommended two-minute brushing time on iPhone. Choose from different songs and sounds to transform dental hygiene from a chore into an enjoyable daily habit that the whole family looks forward to.

Dentists recommend brushing for at least two minutes, but most people fall short without a timer. Toomy solves this by making the two-minute duration engaging rather than tedious. Music plays for the full brushing time, and fun animations provide visual feedback that keeps kids (and kids at heart) entertained.

Brushing streak tracking adds a gamification element that encourages consistency. Build a streak of daily brushing sessions and celebrate the habit of good dental hygiene. Multiple song and sound options keep the experience fresh so it does not become monotonous.

## Who is Toomy for?

- Parents wanting to make tooth brushing fun and consistent for kids
- Children who resist brushing or rush through it
- Adults who want a two-minute brushing timer with entertainment
- Families building good dental hygiene habits together
- Anyone who wants to brush for the full recommended two minutes on iPhone

## Why choose Toomy?

Toomy turns the daily brushing struggle into a positive experience. For kids especially, the music and animations transform a resisted activity into something they actively enjoy, solving one of the most common parenting friction points.

Streak tracking builds long-term consistency by adding positive reinforcement to the habit. It is the most effective way to ensure proper brushing duration while making dental hygiene enjoyable on iOS.
