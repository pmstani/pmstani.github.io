## About Auto Scroll

Auto Scroll is a Safari extension for macOS that automatically scrolls web pages at a customizable speed. Perfect for reading long articles, following recipes while cooking, or browsing hands-free while multitasking — the extension takes over the scrolling so you can focus on the content.

Reading long-form content on the web often means constant scrolling, which is distracting and tiring. Auto Scroll eliminates this by smoothly advancing the page at a speed you choose. Adjust the speed to match your reading pace, and pause or resume with a single click or keyboard shortcut.

The hands-free operation makes Auto Scroll especially valuable when your hands are busy — following a recipe in the kitchen, reading reference material while writing, or reviewing content during a meeting. Set it and focus on what matters.

## Who is Auto Scroll for?

- Readers of long articles who want smooth, hands-free scrolling
- Cooks following online recipes who need their hands free
- Multitaskers reading web content while working on other tasks
- Anyone who finds manual scrolling tedious during long reads
- Mac users seeking a more comfortable reading experience in Safari

## Why choose Auto Scroll?

Auto Scroll delivers smooth, consistent page advancement that is more comfortable than manual scrolling. The adjustable speed control ensures the pace matches your reading speed exactly, and keyboard shortcuts let you pause or adjust without reaching for the mouse.

The hands-free capability opens up use cases that are simply not possible with manual scrolling. It is the most practical reading aid for long-form web content on Safari for macOS.
