## About Day Progress

Day Progress shows you how much of your day, week, month, and year has elapsed with beautiful progress bar widgets on your iPhone home screen and lock screen. By making the passage of time visual, it helps you stay mindful and make the most of every moment.

Time passes whether you are aware of it or not. Day Progress makes that passage visible, turning an abstract concept into a concrete visual that motivates productivity and intentional living. Watch your day's progress bar fill up and feel motivated to accomplish what matters before it reaches 100%.

Customizable widget styles let you match the look to your home screen aesthetic. Choose from different designs, colors, and widget sizes to display the time blocks that matter most to you — whether that is today's remaining hours, this week's progress, or how far into the year you are.

## Who is Day Progress for?

- Productivity-focused individuals who want visual time awareness
- Anyone who wants to be more mindful about how they spend their time
- Widget enthusiasts looking for useful, beautiful home screen additions
- Goal-oriented people tracking time against deadlines and milestones
- iPhone users who want motivational reminders to make the most of each day

## Why choose Day Progress?

Day Progress provides a unique form of motivation through time visualization. Seeing a progress bar for your day, week, or year creates a gentle urgency that is more effective than traditional reminders or to-do lists.

The widget-first design means the information is always visible without opening an app. Beautiful, customizable styles ensure it enhances rather than clutters your home screen on iOS.
