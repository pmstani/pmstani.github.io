## About Is it Down

Is it Down is a Safari extension for macOS that instantly checks whether a website is down for everyone or just you. When a page will not load, one click tells you whether the problem is on your end or with the website's servers, saving you the frustration of troubleshooting a problem you cannot fix.

The extension checks the target website from multiple server locations around the world, providing a reliable answer about the site's status. If the site is down globally, you know to wait. If it is up for everyone else, you know to check your own network, DNS, or VPN settings.

Status history and uptime monitoring let you track a website's reliability over time. Whether you rely on a critical service for work or just want to know why your favorite site is not loading, Is it Down provides the answer instantly from the Safari toolbar.

## Who is Is it Down for?

- Anyone who encounters a website that will not load and wants to know why
- IT professionals diagnosing connectivity issues
- Remote workers who depend on web services for their jobs
- Website operators monitoring their own site's availability
- Mac users who want a quick way to check website status

## Why choose Is it Down?

Is it Down answers the most common question people have when a website fails: "Is it just me?" The instant check from multiple server locations provides a definitive answer in seconds, eliminating guesswork and wasted troubleshooting time.

The Safari toolbar integration means the check is always one click away. Status history adds ongoing value by tracking reliability over time on macOS.
