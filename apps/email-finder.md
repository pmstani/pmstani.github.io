## About Email Finder

Email Finder is a Safari extension for macOS that scans any web page and extracts all email addresses found in the content. With a single click, it identifies every email on the page and presents them in a clean, organized list ready to copy or export.

For sales professionals, recruiters, and marketers, finding contact information on websites is a daily task that can consume significant time. Email Finder automates this process, scanning page content including hidden mailto links and extracting every email address it finds. Results are automatically filtered and deduplicated so you get a clean contact list.

One-click copy to clipboard makes it easy to transfer extracted emails to your CRM, email client, or spreadsheet. The extension works on any website in Safari, making it a versatile lead generation and contact discovery tool.

## Who is Email Finder for?

- Sales professionals gathering leads and contact information from company websites
- Recruiters finding candidate or company email addresses
- Marketers building outreach lists from web content
- Journalists researching contact details for sources
- Anyone who needs to quickly extract email addresses from web pages on Mac

## Why choose Email Finder?

Email Finder turns a tedious manual process into a one-click action. Instead of scanning pages visually and copying emails one by one, the extension automatically finds and organizes every email address on the page.

The automatic deduplication and export features make it production-ready for professional use. It is the most efficient way to extract contact information from websites in Safari on macOS.
