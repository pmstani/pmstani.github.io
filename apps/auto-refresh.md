## About Auto Refresh

Auto Refresh is a Safari extension for macOS that automatically reloads web pages at customizable intervals. Whether you are monitoring live sports scores, tracking auction bids, watching stock tickers, or waiting for content updates, this extension keeps your pages fresh without manual intervention.

Set different refresh intervals per tab based on your needs — a stock ticker might need updates every few seconds while a news page might only need refreshing every few minutes. The visual countdown timer shows you exactly when the next refresh will occur, and you can pause and resume refreshing at any time.

The extension runs quietly in the background, keeping your monitored pages up to date while you focus on other work. It is the simplest way to stay current with dynamic web content without constantly hitting the refresh button.

## Who is Auto Refresh for?

- Sports fans monitoring live scores and game updates
- Online auction participants watching bid activity
- Stock traders tracking market data on web platforms
- Web developers testing page behavior and caching
- Anyone who needs web pages to stay current automatically on Mac

## Why choose Auto Refresh?

Auto Refresh provides per-tab control that lets you customize refresh rates for different types of content. The visual countdown timer gives you awareness of refresh timing without requiring your attention.

The pause and resume functionality means you can quickly stop refreshing when you need to interact with a page, then restart monitoring with a single click. It is the most flexible auto-reload tool for Safari on macOS.
