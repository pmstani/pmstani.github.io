## About Wave & Surf reports by Kooks

Wave & Surf reports by Kooks is the surfing forecast app that keeps you informed about ocean conditions before you head to the beach. Get real-time wave reports, swell data, wind conditions, tide charts, and multi-day surf forecasts for surf spots around the world.

Knowing when conditions are right can make the difference between an epic session and a wasted trip. This app provides all the data surfers need — wave height, swell period, wind direction, and tide information — presented in a clear, easy-to-read format designed for quick pre-session checks.

With a global surf spot directory, you can explore conditions at your local breaks or discover new spots when traveling. Multi-day forecasts help you plan your week around the best conditions, ensuring you never miss a good swell.

## Who is Wave & Surf by Kooks for?

- Surfers who want reliable wave and tide forecasts before sessions
- Beginner surfers learning to read ocean conditions
- Surf travelers exploring new spots around the world
- Ocean sports enthusiasts who need accurate marine weather data
- Anyone who lives near the coast and wants to track surf conditions

## Why choose Wave & Surf by Kooks?

This app is built by surfers for surfers, presenting complex ocean data in a format that is immediately useful. You do not need a meteorology degree to understand the forecasts — the interface translates raw data into actionable surfing information.

The worldwide surf spot directory and multi-day forecasting make it versatile for both local sessions and surf travel. It is the most practical surf forecast tool on iOS for planning your time in the water.
