## About Translator Safari Extension

Translator Safari Extension provides instant translation of web pages and selected text right within Safari on macOS. Whether you are reading a foreign news site, shopping on an international store, or researching in another language, this extension makes any content accessible in your preferred language.

The extension supports dozens of languages and features automatic language detection, so you do not need to know what language a page is in before translating. Translate an entire web page with one click or select specific text passages for targeted translation — both approaches deliver fast, accurate results.

For multilingual users, researchers, and international professionals, this extension removes the language barrier from web browsing. No more copying text to external translation services — everything happens inline within Safari for a seamless reading experience.

## Who is Translator Safari Extension for?

- Multilingual users who browse websites in different languages
- International shoppers reading product descriptions in foreign languages
- Researchers accessing academic content published in other languages
- Travelers planning trips and reading local websites
- Anyone who encounters foreign language content while browsing on Mac

## Why choose Translator Safari Extension?

This extension brings translation directly into your browsing workflow. Instead of copying text to Google Translate or another service, you get instant inline translations without leaving the page you are reading.

Automatic language detection, support for dozens of languages, and the ability to translate both full pages and selected text make it the most versatile translation tool for Safari on macOS.
