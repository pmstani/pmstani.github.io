## About VetKit

VetKit is the offline-first pet health journal that keeps your vaccination records, medication schedules, vet visit notes, and weight logs organized and accessible. Built for reliability, VetKit works without an account, without a server, and without a subscription — just open the app and everything is there.

Your data stays on your device and syncs seamlessly via iCloud across all your Apple devices. No login required, no server dependency, no downtime. VetKit supports dogs, cats, birds, rabbits, reptiles, fish, horses, and custom species, with dedicated profiles for every member of your family.

From vaccination tracking with smart reminders and pre-filled vaccines by species, to medication scheduling with reliable local notifications and dose confirmation, VetKit gives you a complete picture of each pet's health history. Log vet visits with diagnosis, treatment notes, and cost tracking. Attach photos of test results or prescriptions. Track weight trends over time with built-in charts.

## Who is VetKit for?

- Pet owners who want a reliable, always-available health record for their animals
- Multi-pet households managing different vaccination and medication schedules
- Dog and cat owners tracking weight trends, medications, and vet visits
- Owners of exotic pets (birds, reptiles, rabbits, fish, horses) who need organized health records
- Anyone who wants to generate a vet-ready PDF health summary for appointments

## Why choose VetKit?

VetKit stands out with its offline-first architecture — your pet's health records are always available, even without internet. The app generates comprehensive PDF health summaries with pet profiles, vaccination history, current medications, recent visits, weight charts, and health notes that you can share via email, AirDrop, or print for your next vet appointment.

Smart vaccination reminders notify you 1 week, 3 days, or 1 day before a vaccine is due, so you never miss a shot. The health note journal lets you track behavior changes, diet changes, symptoms, injuries, and grooming with photo attachments and instant search. Everything syncs across your Apple devices via iCloud automatically.

One-time purchase. No hidden costs. No paywall. No subscription. Just a complete pet health journal that works.
