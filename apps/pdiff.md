## About PDIFF

PDIFF lets you visually compare two PDF documents side by side on macOS, highlighting differences in text, layout, and images. When you need to review document revisions, verify contract changes, or check design proofs, PDIFF makes every difference immediately visible.

Manual PDF comparison is error-prone and exhausting. Subtle text changes, moved paragraphs, and adjusted formatting can easily be missed when scanning documents by eye. PDIFF automates this process, detecting and highlighting every difference between two versions so nothing slips through.

The visual highlighting uses clear color coding to distinguish additions, deletions, and modifications. Export comparison reports to share findings with colleagues or maintain an audit trail of document changes. PDIFF turns a tedious review process into a quick, reliable check.

## Who is PDIFF for?

- Lawyers and legal professionals reviewing contract revisions
- Editors comparing document drafts and revisions
- Designers verifying print proofs against original designs
- Business professionals reviewing updated proposals and agreements
- Anyone who needs to accurately compare two PDF versions on Mac

## Why choose PDIFF?

PDIFF provides visual accuracy that human eyes alone cannot match. Every text change, layout shift, and image modification is detected and highlighted, eliminating the risk of overlooking subtle but important differences.

The export capability makes PDIFF suitable for professional workflows where documentation of changes is required. It is the most reliable PDF comparison tool available on macOS.
