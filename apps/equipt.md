## About Equipt

Equipt helps you track and manage your equipment, gear, and physical assets on iPhone. Log purchase dates, warranty information, maintenance schedules, and condition for all your tools, electronics, sports gear, and professional equipment in one organized catalog.

Keeping track of warranties, maintenance dates, and equipment details is something most people try to do with receipts in drawers and notes on scraps of paper. Equipt replaces that mess with a clean digital catalog where every piece of equipment has its own profile — complete with photos, specifications, and important dates.

Maintenance schedule reminders ensure you never miss routine servicing or warranty expiration dates. Photo documentation for each item creates a visual record that is useful for insurance, resale, and troubleshooting. Whether you manage a few expensive items or a large gear collection, Equipt keeps everything organized.

## Who is Equipt for?

- Professionals tracking tools and work equipment
- Outdoor enthusiasts managing sports and adventure gear
- Homeowners logging appliances, warranties, and maintenance
- Photographers and creators tracking expensive equipment
- Anyone with valuable gear who wants organized asset management on iPhone

## Why choose Equipt?

Equipt provides structured equipment management that scattered notes and receipts cannot match. Warranty tracking, maintenance reminders, and photo documentation create a complete profile for every item you own.

The proactive reminder system ensures maintenance happens on schedule and warranties are used before they expire. It is the most organized way to manage your equipment and gear on iOS.
