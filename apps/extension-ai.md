## About Extension AI

Extension AI brings artificial intelligence directly into your Safari browsing experience on macOS. Summarize long articles, translate content in any language, generate text, and get AI-powered assistance on any webpage — all without switching to a separate app or tab.

The power of AI is most useful when it is available right where you need it. Extension AI integrates into Safari so that AI capabilities are always one click away from the content you are reading. Highlight text to get a summary, translation, or rewrite. Ask questions about the page content and get context-aware answers.

Whether you are researching, studying, or working, Extension AI acts as an intelligent companion that enhances your productivity across every website. The extension processes content directly on the page, providing a seamless experience that feels like a natural part of browsing.

## Who is Extension AI for?

- Researchers who need to quickly summarize and analyze web content
- Multilingual users wanting instant translation within Safari
- Writers seeking AI-powered text generation and rewriting tools
- Students who want AI assistance while studying online materials
- Professionals who want browser-integrated AI without switching apps

## Why choose Extension AI?

Extension AI eliminates the friction of copying text to a separate AI tool. By embedding AI capabilities directly in Safari, it saves time and keeps you in your browsing flow. The context-aware assistance means the AI understands the page you are on and can provide more relevant responses.

Text summarization, translation, and generation all work inline on any webpage. It is the most convenient way to access AI assistance while browsing on macOS.
