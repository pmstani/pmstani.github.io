## About Dyslexia

Dyslexia is a Safari extension for macOS that transforms web text into a more readable format for people with dyslexia. By applying proven typographic adjustments — including the OpenDyslexic font, increased letter spacing, and optimized font weight — it makes online reading accessible and comfortable across every website you visit.

Reading on the web can be particularly challenging for dyslexic users because most websites use standard fonts that are not designed with reading disabilities in mind. This extension solves that problem with a single click. Activate it from the Safari toolbar and watch any webpage transform into a dyslexia-friendly reading experience with carefully tuned text formatting.

The extension offers customizable settings so you can adjust letter spacing, word spacing, and text formatting to match your personal preferences. Whether you have mild or severe dyslexia, you can fine-tune the reading experience to what works best for you.

## Who is Dyslexia for?

- People with dyslexia who find standard web text difficult to read
- Parents and educators supporting dyslexic students who use Safari
- Accessibility advocates looking for practical browser tools
- Anyone who benefits from increased letter spacing and clearer fonts
- Mac users who want a more comfortable reading experience online

## Why choose Dyslexia?

This Safari extension applies research-backed typographic changes that are proven to improve readability for dyslexic readers. The OpenDyslexic font, combined with optimized spacing, creates a dramatically different reading experience compared to standard web fonts.

One-click activation from the Safari toolbar means there is no friction in using it. You do not need to configure anything for each site — just turn it on and browse normally. The accessibility improvements apply universally, making the entire web more readable on your Mac.
