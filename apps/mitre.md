## About Mitre

Mitre is picture-framing software for custom frame shops, galleries, photographers and artists, built natively for iPhone, iPad and Mac. It replaces the spreadsheet quote and the clunky Windows point-of-sale with a fast, beautiful, fully offline framing studio that prices a job and shows the customer exactly what they'll get.

Choose the artwork size, the matting, the glass and the moulding, and Mitre instantly draws a to-scale preview of the finished piece — real moulding profiles, single or double mat reveals, glazing sheen, and the customer's own photo dropped inside the frame. At the same moment it prices the job from your own catalog: moulding footage, united-inch matting, glass area, backing and labour, every line itemised.

When the customer says yes, one tap produces a branded quote PDF with the proof, and a shop cut-sheet listing every measurement the bench needs — glass size, mat openings, frame outer size and moulding chop length.

## Who is Mitre for?

- Custom picture-framing shops that quote jobs at the counter
- Galleries and print studios pricing framed editions
- Photographers and artists who sell their work framed
- Anyone who needs fast, consistent, profitable framing estimates without a subscription

## Why choose Mitre?

Most framing software is either an expensive Windows point-of-sale system or a hobbyist size calculator. Mitre is neither. It is a professional quoting tool that runs entirely on your device, prices from your own moulding, mat and glazing catalog, and produces a true-to-life proof that helps close the sale.

There is no subscription, no account and no cloud — your prices and your customers never leave your device, and the app works with zero signal on the shop floor, at an art fair, or in a client's home. Mitre is designed to look as good as the frames you sell: a calm, gallery-grade interface with measurements you can trust.

Price any custom frame in seconds, hand your customer a proof they can picture on their wall, and give your bench an exact cut-sheet — all from one beautiful app.
