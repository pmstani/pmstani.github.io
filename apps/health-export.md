## About Health Export

Health Export lets you export your Apple Health data in various formats including CSV, JSON, and PDF on iPhone. Back up your health metrics, share data with healthcare providers, and analyze your health trends with your own tools — giving you full control over the health data your iPhone collects.

Apple Health collects extensive data about your activity, heart rate, sleep, nutrition, and more, but accessing and using that data outside the Health app is limited. Health Export unlocks your data by letting you export specific metrics in standard formats that work with spreadsheets, data analysis tools, and medical records systems.

Select specific health metrics and custom date ranges for targeted exports. Share reports directly with healthcare providers who need your health history, or export everything for personal backup and analysis. Your health data is yours — Health Export ensures you can use it however you need.

## Who is Health Export for?

- Anyone who wants to back up their Apple Health data
- People sharing health metrics with doctors and healthcare providers
- Data enthusiasts who want to analyze their health trends in spreadsheets
- Fitness trackers who need detailed export of workout and activity data
- Users who want a portable backup of their health records on iPhone

## Why choose Health Export?

Health Export provides the data portability that Apple Health lacks natively. Multiple export formats ensure your data works with whatever tool you need — CSV for spreadsheets, JSON for developers, and PDF for sharing with medical professionals.

Custom date range filtering and metric selection mean you export exactly what you need, not everything. It is the most flexible way to access and share your Apple Health data on iOS.
