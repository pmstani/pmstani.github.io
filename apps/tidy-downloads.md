## About Tidy Downloads

Tidy Downloads automatically organizes your messy Downloads folder on macOS by sorting files into categorized subfolders. Set custom rules to keep your downloads tidy and find files faster without the tedium of manual cleanup.

The Downloads folder is one of the messiest places on most computers. PDFs, images, documents, archives, and installers pile up into an unsearchable mess. Tidy Downloads solves this by automatically filing new downloads into organized subfolders based on file type, name patterns, or custom rules you define.

Scheduled cleanup tasks keep your Downloads folder organized over time, not just as a one-time fix. Smart file categorization handles the sorting automatically, so you never need to think about where a file should go — it lands in the right place every time.

## Who is Tidy Downloads for?

- Mac users with cluttered, unmanageable Downloads folders
- Anyone who downloads many files and struggles to find them later
- Professionals who want automatic file organization without manual effort
- People who have tried to organize their Downloads manually but cannot keep up
- Users who want scheduled, recurring folder cleanup on macOS

Tidy Downloads automates what would otherwise be a tedious, ongoing chore. Custom rules mean the organization matches your specific needs, not a one-size-fits-all system. The automatic sorting happens transparently, requiring no daily effort from you.

Scheduled cleanup ensures your Downloads folder stays organized permanently. It is the most effective way to end Downloads folder chaos on macOS.
