## About Make It Dark Mode

Make It Dark Mode is a Safari extension for macOS that applies a dark theme to any website, reducing eye strain during nighttime browsing sessions. Even websites that do not offer a built-in dark mode become comfortable to read with this extension's intelligent theme engine.

The extension lets you customize darkness levels and accent colors to create the perfect dark theme for your eyes. Whether you prefer a true black background for OLED-like contrast or a softer dark gray, you can fine-tune the appearance across every website. Per-site preferences let you whitelist sites that already have their own dark mode.

Automatic scheduling based on time of day means you can have dark mode activate at sunset and deactivate at sunrise, matching your natural viewing patterns. No manual toggling required — just set your preferences and browse comfortably at any hour.

## Who is Make It Dark Mode for?

- Mac users who browse the web at night and want to reduce eye strain
- Anyone who prefers dark interfaces but visits sites without dark mode support
- People sensitive to bright screens who need lower contrast for comfortable reading
- Developers and designers who work late hours in Safari
- Users who want automatic dark mode scheduling on macOS

## Why choose Make It Dark Mode?

Make It Dark Mode provides universal dark mode coverage that goes beyond what macOS system settings can achieve. While system-level dark mode only affects supported apps, this extension transforms every website in Safari into a comfortable dark reading experience.

The combination of customizable darkness levels, per-site preferences, and automatic time-based scheduling means the extension works exactly the way you want with zero daily effort. It is the most flexible dark mode solution for Safari on Mac.
