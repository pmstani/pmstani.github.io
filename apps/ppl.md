## About PPL Théorique

PPL Théorique is the premium study companion for the theoretical exam of the **PPL(A)** — the *licence de pilote privé avion* (Private Pilot Licence, Aeroplanes) — built to the **EASA** standard and the **DGAC** exam format used in France. Every question, every lesson and every explanation is anchored in the official EASA learning objectives, so what you revise is exactly what you are tested on.

The app turns a dense theory syllabus into an active learning path. It ships with **579 original multiple-choice questions (QCM)** covering the **9 EASA subjects** — Air Law, Aircraft General Knowledge, Flight Performance and Planning, Human Performance, Meteorology, Navigation, Operational Procedures, Principles of Flight, and VFR Communications — each accompanied by a clear explanation. A dedicated **Training mode** lets you work subject by subject at your own pace, while a **mock exam in the DGAC format** reproduces the real conditions of exam day, including the **75 % pass mark per subject**.

A **spaced-repetition** engine schedules your reviews at the right moment, and an **errors mode** re-tests precisely what you get wrong. Alongside the QCM, **85 original, clearly structured lessons** give you the underlying theory, with instant search, a glossary and bookmarks. An *"are you ready?"* dashboard measures your mastery, your syllabus coverage and your real level of preparation, so you always know what is left to revise.

PPL Théorique is a **single purchase, for life** — no subscription, no in-app purchases, no ads. You download it once and everything works **100 % offline**: in a waiting room, on the train, or far from any network, your preparation follows you everywhere. Your progress and exam history stay on your device.

## Who is PPL Théorique for?

- Student pilots preparing the theoretical exam of the **PPL(A)** in an aéro-club or flight school
- Candidates who want to sit the **DGAC** theory exam and pass on the first attempt
- Trainees working toward the **LAPL(A)** who share most of the same theoretical syllabus
- Pilots returning to flying who need to refresh Air Law, Meteorology and Navigation
- Anyone who prefers to revise **offline**, on short commutes or without a reliable connection
- Instructors and aéro-clubs looking for a reliable, up-to-date revision tool for their students

## Why choose PPL Théorique?

PPL Théorique is built strictly around the **official EASA syllabus** and the **DGAC exam format**, so the QCM you practise mirror the structure, difficulty and subject split of the real exam. The mock exam applies the same **75 % per-subject rule** as the official test, which trains you to reach a passing level in *every* subject rather than compensating a weak topic with a strong one — the single most common cause of failure.

The learning method is demanding and honest, designed like a real flight school. **Spaced repetition** surfaces the questions you are most likely to forget, the **errors mode** relentlessly re-tests your weak spots, and the **85 lessons** let you go back to the underlying theory whenever a QCM reveals a gap. The *"are you ready?"* dashboard turns your practice into a clear readiness signal, so you sit the exam knowing you are ready — not hoping you are.

Because everything runs **locally and offline**, you can revise anywhere: between two flights at the club, on a train with no signal, or the evening before the exam in airplane mode. There is **no subscription and no advertising** — one fair purchase unlocks the whole app for life, and updates keep it aligned with the EASA objectives. Just you and the syllabus, in your pocket, until the day you walk in confident and pass on the first try.
