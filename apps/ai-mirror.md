## About AI Mirror

AI Mirror transforms your iPhone into a convenient pocket mirror with AI-enhanced features that go beyond a simple camera preview. Use it for quick appearance checks with smart lighting adjustment that compensates for poor ambient conditions, plus zoom and freeze frame for detailed viewing.

The lock screen widget provides instant access — no need to unlock your phone and open an app. A quick tap on the widget launches your mirror immediately, perfect for those moments when you need a fast check before a meeting, date, or photo.

AI-powered lighting adjustment ensures you see an accurate reflection regardless of your environment. Whether you are in a dimly lit restaurant or harsh fluorescent lighting, the AI compensates to show you a natural, true-to-life view. Freeze frame lets you capture a moment and examine details without holding still.

AI Mirror goes beyond a basic front camera preview by adding intelligent lighting compensation and practical features like freeze frame and zoom. The lock screen widget makes it the fastest-access mirror available on iPhone.
