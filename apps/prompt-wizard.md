## About Prompt Wizard

Prompt Wizard is a Safari extension for macOS that helps you craft better prompts for AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, and other language models. Access a curated library of proven prompt templates and insert them directly into AI chat interfaces with a single click, saving time and getting better results.

The quality of your AI output depends heavily on the quality of your input. Prompt Wizard solves the prompt engineering challenge by providing tested templates across categories like writing, coding, analysis, creativity, and business. Instead of spending time crafting the perfect prompt from scratch, start with a proven framework and customize it to your needs.

Create and save your own custom prompts alongside the curated library. Over time, you build a personal collection of prompt templates that work perfectly for your specific use cases, making every AI interaction more efficient and productive.

## Who is Prompt Wizard for?

- Regular users of ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI assistants
- Professionals who want to improve the quality of their AI outputs
- Content creators using AI tools for writing and ideation
- Developers leveraging AI for coding assistance
- Anyone who wants to save time on prompt engineering on Mac

## Why choose Prompt Wizard?

Prompt Wizard eliminates the trial and error of prompt engineering. The curated template library represents proven approaches that consistently produce better AI responses, saving you the effort of figuring out what works through experimentation.

The one-click insertion directly into AI chat interfaces makes it the most convenient prompt tool available. Combined with custom prompt saving, it grows more valuable the more you use it, becoming your personal AI productivity multiplier on macOS.
