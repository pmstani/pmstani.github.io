## About Site Slurpr

Site Slurpr is a Safari extension for macOS that downloads entire websites for offline viewing, preserving the full structure, pages, navigation, and assets. When you need to archive a website, access content offline, or back up web resources before they disappear, Site Slurpr captures everything.

Unlike single-page download tools, Site Slurpr follows links and downloads multiple pages to create a complete offline copy of a website. The preserved site structure means you can navigate the downloaded site just as you would the live version — clicking links, moving between pages, and browsing naturally.

Selective page downloading lets you choose which sections of a site to capture, saving time and disk space when you only need specific parts. The result is a local archive that is fully functional, portable, and immune to the original site going offline.

- Anyone preparing for offline access to multi-page web content
- Digital archivists preserving websites before they change or disappear

Site Slurpr goes beyond single-page saving to capture entire websites with working navigation. The offline copies are fully browsable, maintaining the same user experience as the live site.

Selective downloading gives you control over scope and storage. Whether you need an entire site or just a specific section, Site Slurpr adapts to your needs, making it the most capable website archiving tool for Safari on macOS.
