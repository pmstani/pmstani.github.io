## About Read Easier

Read Easier is a Safari extension for macOS that applies the Atkinson Hyperlegible font to any website, making web content significantly more readable. Developed by the Braille Institute, the Atkinson Hyperlegible typeface is specifically designed to improve character distinction for people with low vision, and this extension brings it to every website you visit.

Standard web fonts often use characters that are too similar — lowercase l, uppercase I, and the number 1 can look nearly identical. The Atkinson Hyperlegible font addresses this by maximizing the visual differences between each character, reducing reading errors and fatigue for people with vision challenges.

Customize font size and spacing to match your personal comfort level, and toggle the extension on or off from the Safari toolbar with a single click. The result is a web browsing experience that is dramatically more readable without altering the overall page layout.

## Who is Read Easier for?

- People with low vision who struggle to read standard web fonts
- Anyone experiencing eye fatigue from extended web reading
- Accessibility advocates looking for practical reading improvement tools
- Older adults who find modern web typography difficult to read
- Mac users who want a clearer, more legible browsing experience

## Why choose Read Easier?

Read Easier brings a scientifically designed accessibility font to the entire web. The Atkinson Hyperlegible font is not just another font — it was created specifically to be maximally readable for people with vision challenges, making it the gold standard for accessible typography.

The instant toggle and customizable settings make it practical for daily use. Whether you need it all the time or just for certain reading-heavy sites, Read Easier adapts to your needs on macOS.
