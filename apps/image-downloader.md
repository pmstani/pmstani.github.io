## About Image Downloader

Image Downloader is a Safari extension for macOS that lets you bulk download all images from any webpage with a single click. Instead of right-clicking and saving images one at a time, this extension finds every image on the page and lets you download them all at once.

Filter images by size, format, and resolution to quickly target exactly what you need. Preview images before downloading to ensure you are saving the right content, and choose a custom destination folder for organized storage. Whether you need high-resolution photos, product images, or design assets, the filtering tools help you cut through thumbnails and icons to find the images that matter.

The extension works on any website in Safari, making it invaluable for designers collecting references, researchers gathering visual materials, or anyone who needs to save multiple images from the web efficiently.

## Why choose Image Downloader?

Image Downloader replaces the tedious right-click-save-repeat workflow with a single bulk action. The filtering and preview features ensure you only download what you actually need, saving time and disk space.

The ability to filter by size and format is particularly powerful — it lets you skip small thumbnails and navigation icons to focus on the substantial images you are looking for. It is the most efficient image-saving tool for Safari on macOS.
