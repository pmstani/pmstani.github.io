## About Snapmark

Snapmark combines the power of screenshots with bookmarking, letting you capture visual snapshots of web pages and save them for later on both iOS and macOS. Instead of relying on text-based bookmark lists, you get a visual library of saved pages that makes finding and remembering content effortless.

Traditional bookmarks are just URLs and titles — easy to forget and hard to scan. Snapmark changes this by capturing a screenshot alongside each bookmark, creating a rich visual record of the content you save. Perfect for research, shopping comparisons, design inspiration, and keeping track of web content that matters to you.

Organize your visual bookmarks with folders and tags, and quickly share or export them when needed. The visual approach makes Snapmark especially powerful for tasks where seeing the content is faster than reading titles, like comparing products or reviewing design references.

## Who is Snapmark for?

- Researchers building visual reference collections from the web
- Online shoppers comparing products across multiple sites
- Designers saving web inspiration and visual references
- Students organizing research sources with visual context
- Anyone frustrated with text-only bookmark lists on iPhone and Mac

## Why choose Snapmark?

Snapmark's visual-first approach solves the fundamental problem with traditional bookmarks — you cannot remember what they link to. By pairing each saved page with a screenshot, you can scan your collection visually and find what you need instantly.

Available on both iOS and macOS, Snapmark works wherever you browse. The folder and tag organization system keeps even large collections manageable, making it the most intuitive way to save and revisit web content.
