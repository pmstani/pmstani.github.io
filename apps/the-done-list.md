## About The Done List

The Done List flips traditional to-do lists by focusing on what you have accomplished rather than what remains undone. Log your daily achievements, build a visual scrapbook of progress, and celebrate your productivity on iPhone — because what you have done matters just as much as what is left.

To-do lists can create anxiety by constantly highlighting unfinished work. The Done List takes the opposite approach: at the end of each day, log what you accomplished and build a growing record of your productivity. Over time, this creates a powerful visual history of everything you have achieved.

Weekly and monthly progress reviews help you see patterns in your productivity and recognize how much you actually get done. Streak tracking for consistent logging adds a gamification element that encourages daily reflection on your accomplishments.

## Who is The Done List for?

- Anyone who feels overwhelmed by traditional to-do lists
- People who want to recognize and celebrate their daily accomplishments
- Professionals tracking what they achieve for performance reviews
- Individuals building a journaling or reflection habit
- Users who are motivated by progress tracking and streaks on iPhone

## Why choose The Done List?

The Done List provides a psychologically healthier approach to productivity tracking. By celebrating accomplishments instead of stressing over incomplete tasks, it builds motivation and self-confidence rather than anxiety.

The visual scrapbook format makes reviewing your achievements genuinely enjoyable. Combined with streak tracking, it creates a positive feedback loop that encourages consistent reflection on iOS.
