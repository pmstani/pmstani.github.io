## About Food Scanner for Safari

Food Scanner for Safari is an extension that analyzes nutritional information on grocery and food product websites while you shop online. Get instant nutrition grades, ingredient alerts, and healthier alternative suggestions without leaving the page — making informed food choices easier than ever.

Online grocery shopping is increasingly popular, but comparing nutritional quality across products is tedious. Food Scanner automates this by reading product pages and delivering clear nutrition scores, highlighting concerning ingredients, and warning you about additives, allergens, and high sugar or sodium content.

When the scanner identifies potential issues with a product, it suggests healthier alternatives you can consider. This proactive approach helps you gradually improve your diet without spending hours comparing nutrition labels manually.

## Who is Food Scanner for Safari for?

- Health-conscious online grocery shoppers
- Parents checking nutritional quality of food for their families
- People managing dietary restrictions or food sensitivities
- Anyone wanting to make better food choices while shopping online
- Fitness enthusiasts tracking macronutrient quality on Mac

## Why choose Food Scanner for Safari?

Food Scanner brings nutritional intelligence directly into your online shopping workflow. Instead of manually reading and comparing nutrition labels, you get instant analysis and grades that make healthy choices obvious.

The healthier alternative suggestions transform the extension from a passive analyzer into an active health advisor. It is the most practical way to improve your diet through smarter online grocery shopping on macOS.
