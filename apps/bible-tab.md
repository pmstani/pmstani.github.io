## About Bible Tab

Bible Tab is a Safari extension for macOS that transforms every new browser tab into an inspiring Bible verse. For Christians who want to weave daily scripture into their routine, this extension delivers curated verses alongside beautiful background imagery each time you open a new tab.

Instead of staring at a blank tab or a list of frequently visited sites, Bible Tab fills that moment with meaningful content. Each new tab presents a carefully selected verse from multiple Bible translations, paired with stunning photography that creates a contemplative atmosphere right in your browser.

You can bookmark your favorite verses for easy reference and revisit them whenever you need encouragement. The extension integrates seamlessly into Safari on macOS, requiring no extra effort to use — simply browse as you normally would, and let God's word find you throughout the day.

## Who is Bible Tab for?

- Christians seeking daily scripture reminders woven into their routine
- Mac users who want to start each browsing session with inspiration
- Bible study groups looking for a simple way to encounter new verses
- Anyone interested in replacing empty new tabs with meaningful content
- Church leaders wanting to recommend an easy devotional tool

## Why choose Bible Tab?

Bible Tab meets you where you already are — in your browser. There is no separate app to open, no reminder to dismiss. Every new tab naturally presents scripture, making it effortless to engage with the Bible throughout your workday.

With support for multiple Bible translations and a curated selection of verses, Bible Tab ensures variety and depth. The beautiful background imagery enhances the experience, turning a mundane browser action into a moment of reflection and peace on your Mac.
