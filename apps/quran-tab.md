## About Quran Tab

Quran Tab is a Safari extension for macOS that displays a Quran verse on every new tab you open, providing daily spiritual reminders for Muslim users. Each verse is presented with beautiful Arabic calligraphy alongside English translations, creating a meaningful connection to your faith throughout your browsing day.

For Muslims who want to stay close to the Quran without disrupting their workflow, this extension integrates seamlessly into daily computer use. Every new tab becomes an opportunity for reflection, presenting verses from across the Quran in both their original Arabic text and accessible English translation.

Beautiful calligraphy and backgrounds enhance the presentation, making each verse a visual and spiritual experience. Bookmark and share your favorite verses with family and friends, building a personal collection of the ayat that resonate most deeply with you.

## Who is Quran Tab for?

- Muslim users who want daily Quran reminders on their Mac
- Anyone seeking to increase their exposure to Quranic verses
- Arabic learners who benefit from seeing Arabic text with translations
- Mac users wanting meaningful content on their new tab page
- Families who want to share inspirational Islamic content

## Why choose Quran Tab?

Quran Tab brings the Quran to where you spend your time — your browser. Without needing to open a separate app, you encounter sacred verses naturally throughout your day, building a consistent connection to Islamic scripture.

The combination of authentic Arabic calligraphy with English translation serves both spiritual and educational purposes. It is the most elegant way to integrate Quranic study into your daily routine on macOS.
