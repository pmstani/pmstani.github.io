## About Tiny Temple

Tiny Temple delivers bite-sized mental health rituals that fit into your busiest days on iPhone. Quick breathing exercises, micro-meditations under five minutes, and daily gratitude prompts help you build a sustainable self-care practice without requiring long sessions or significant time commitments.

The biggest barrier to meditation and self-care is time. Tiny Temple removes that barrier by offering rituals that take just minutes — short enough to fit into a coffee break, a commute pause, or the moments before sleep. Despite their brevity, these micro-practices deliver real benefits for stress reduction, focus, and emotional well-being.

Streak tracking and reminders help you build consistency, which research shows is more important than session length for mental health benefits. Daily gratitude prompts round out the practice by shifting your attention to positive experiences, building resilience and life satisfaction over time.

## Who is Tiny Temple for?

- Busy people who want mental health benefits without long meditation sessions
- Beginners who find traditional meditation intimidating or time-consuming
- Anyone seeking quick stress relief during a hectic day
- People building a daily mindfulness habit for the first time
- Users who want guided breathing exercises on their iPhone

## Why choose Tiny Temple?

Tiny Temple proves that effective self-care does not require lengthy sessions. The micro-ritual approach makes consistent practice achievable for even the busiest schedules, and consistency is what drives real mental health improvement.

The combination of micro-meditations, breathing exercises, and gratitude prompts provides variety within brevity. Streak tracking adds the accountability needed to build a lasting habit on iOS.
