## About CalXPort

CalXPort lets you export your Apple Calendar events and reminders to CSV and Excel on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Whether you need timesheets, meeting logs, billing records, or class schedules, CalXPort turns your calendar into structured, analysis-ready spreadsheet data that works with any tool you already use.

What sets CalXPort apart is its powerful template system. Choose from 10 built-in export templates designed for specific workflows — freelancer timesheets, lawyer billing logs, student class schedules, travel itineraries, and more. For advanced users, the custom template editor supports regex-based field parsing, letting you extract structured data from event titles, notes, and locations automatically.

All processing happens entirely on your device. CalXPort requires no account, no cloud service, and no subscription to export your data. Your calendar information never leaves your iPhone, iPad, or Mac — giving you complete control over sensitive scheduling and billing data.

## Who is CalXPort for?

- Lawyers logging client time and producing billing records
- Students organizing class schedules and exam calendars into spreadsheets
- Travelers compiling itineraries and trip plans for sharing or printing
- Anyone who needs their calendar data in spreadsheet format for reporting or analysis
