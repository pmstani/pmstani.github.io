## About Mental Health by HappySteps

Mental Health by HappySteps is a mood and stress tracking app that helps you understand your emotional patterns and build healthier mental health habits. By logging your daily feelings and stress levels, you create a personal emotional profile that reveals triggers, trends, and opportunities for improvement.

The app goes beyond simple mood logging. It provides guided breathing exercises, relaxation techniques, and mindfulness practices that address the stress and emotional challenges you identify through tracking. Weekly and monthly reports give you a clear picture of your mental health trajectory, helping you see what is working and where to focus.

Building emotional awareness is the first step toward better mental health, and HappySteps makes that process accessible and consistent. The daily logging habit takes just a moment, but the insights it generates can have a profound impact on how you manage your emotional well-being.

HappySteps combines tracking with action. You do not just log your feelings — you get practical exercises and techniques to improve them. This dual approach of awareness plus intervention makes it more effective than standalone mood trackers.
