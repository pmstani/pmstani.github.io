## About CueVoice

CueVoice is a voice-activated teleprompter on iPhone that scrolls your script automatically as you speak. Unlike traditional teleprompters that scroll at a fixed speed, CueVoice listens to your voice and matches the scroll rate to your natural speaking pace — so it waits when you pause and accelerates when you speed up.

For content creators, presenters, and public speakers, maintaining natural delivery while reading from a script is essential. CueVoice eliminates the disconnect between scroll speed and speaking pace that makes other teleprompters awkward to use. Your words control the scrolling, not a timer.

Adjustable scroll speed, customizable text size, and mirror mode for professional teleprompting setups make CueVoice versatile for any presentation scenario. Import scripts from text files to get started quickly, and deliver your content with confidence knowing the prompter will follow your lead.

## Who is CueVoice for?

- Content creators recording video with scripted content
- Public speakers and presenters who want hands-free script reading
- YouTubers and podcasters delivering scripted segments
- Corporate trainers and educators presenting prepared material
- Anyone who needs a teleprompter that follows their speaking pace on iPhone

## Why choose CueVoice?

CueVoice's voice-activated scrolling is a fundamental improvement over fixed-speed teleprompters. By following your natural speaking pace, it eliminates the anxiety of keeping up with or waiting for the scroll — letting you focus entirely on your delivery.

Mirror mode and adjustable text size make it suitable for professional setups. It is the most natural and stress-free teleprompter experience available on iOS.
