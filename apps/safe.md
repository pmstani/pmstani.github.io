## About Safe

Safe helps you create a comprehensive home insurance inventory by cataloging your possessions with photos, descriptions, and estimated values on iPhone. When disaster strikes and you need to file an insurance claim, Safe ensures you have organized documentation of everything you own.

Most people cannot accurately recall their possessions after a fire, flood, or theft — let alone provide the documentation insurance companies require for claims. Safe solves this by giving you a simple way to photograph, describe, and value each item before you need to, creating a definitive record that supports faster, more complete claims.

Organize items by room or category, track purchase dates and values, and export inventory reports formatted for insurance purposes. The process of cataloging takes time upfront but saves enormous stress and financial loss if you ever need to make a claim.

## Who is Safe for?

- Homeowners wanting to document possessions for insurance purposes
- Renters who need proof of belongings for renters insurance claims
- Anyone who wants to be financially prepared for theft or disaster
- People moving to a new home who want to catalog their assets
- Insurance-conscious families protecting their financial interests on iPhone

## Why choose Safe?

Safe provides a structured, insurance-ready approach to home inventory documentation. Photos, descriptions, values, and purchase dates create the comprehensive records that insurance companies need to process claims quickly and fairly.

The export functionality generates reports formatted for insurance submission, saving time and stress during an already difficult situation. It is the most thorough home inventory tool available on iOS.
