## About Clean Genie

Clean Genie is a storage optimization app for iOS that helps you reclaim valuable space on your iPhone by identifying and removing duplicate photos, oversized files, and unnecessary clutter. If your device is running low on storage or feels sluggish, Clean Genie provides intelligent cleanup suggestions to get things running smoothly again.

The app scans your photo library to detect duplicate and similar images that are wasting space, then lets you review and remove them with confidence. It also identifies large files taking up disproportionate storage so you can decide what to keep and what to remove. Every suggestion comes with a preview, so you never accidentally delete something important.

Beyond one-time cleanups, Clean Genie provides storage usage analytics that help you understand where your space is going. With ongoing visibility into your device's storage health, you can keep your iPhone organized and prevent storage issues before they happen.

## Who is Clean Genie for?

- iPhone users running low on storage space
- Anyone with a large photo library full of duplicate images
- People who want a cleaner, faster-running device
- Users tired of manually searching for files to delete
- Anyone who wants ongoing visibility into their storage usage

## Why choose Clean Genie?

Clean Genie takes the guesswork out of freeing up space. Instead of manually scrolling through thousands of photos or checking file sizes one by one, the app's smart detection algorithms find the biggest opportunities for reclaiming storage instantly.

The preview-before-delete approach means you stay in control. Clean Genie never removes anything without your approval, and its storage analytics give you a clear picture of your device's health. It is the simplest way to keep your iPhone optimized on iOS.
