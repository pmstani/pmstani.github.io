## About PanicVault

PanicVault is a native password manager built for iOS and macOS that securely stores your passwords, credentials, and sensitive information with end-to-end encryption. With biometric authentication via Face ID and Touch ID, your data stays safe and accessible only to you — synced seamlessly across all your Apple devices via iCloud.

Unlike web-based password managers that run in browsers, PanicVault is a native application that integrates deeply with iOS and macOS. This means faster performance, better battery efficiency, and tighter integration with system features like AutoFill in Safari and apps. Your encrypted vault lives on your devices and syncs through iCloud, keeping your data within Apple's secure ecosystem.

Beyond passwords, PanicVault stores secure notes, credit card information, and other sensitive credentials. Secure sharing lets you share specific credentials with trusted contacts without exposing your entire vault. Auto-fill in Safari and apps makes logging in seamless — authenticate with Face ID or Touch ID and your credentials are entered automatically.

## Who is PanicVault for?

- Apple users who want a native, non-browser-based password manager
- Anyone seeking end-to-end encrypted credential storage
- People who prefer biometric authentication for accessing passwords
- Users wanting seamless password sync across iPhone and Mac via iCloud
- Privacy-conscious individuals who want their data stored securely on their devices

## Why choose PanicVault?

PanicVault's native architecture delivers advantages that web-based password managers cannot match. Native performance, system-level integration with AutoFill, and iCloud sync create an experience that feels like a built-in Apple feature rather than a third-party tool.

End-to-end encryption ensures even if data were intercepted during sync, it would be unreadable. Biometric authentication means you never type a master password on your devices. It is the most Apple-native password management experience available for iOS and macOS.
