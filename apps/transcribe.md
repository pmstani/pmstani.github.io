## About Transcribe

Transcribe converts speech to text using advanced AI on iPhone, delivering fast and accurate transcriptions of meetings, lectures, interviews, voice memos, and any spoken content. With support for multiple languages, it is a versatile dictation and transcription tool for personal and professional use.

Whether you are recording a meeting, transcribing a lecture, or dictating notes on the go, Transcribe turns your spoken words into editable text quickly and reliably. The AI-powered speech recognition handles natural speech patterns, accents, and varied audio quality, producing clean transcripts that require minimal editing.

Export your transcripts in multiple formats to use them wherever you need — in documents, emails, notes apps, or cloud storage. Real-time transcription lets you see words appear as they are spoken, making it useful for live note-taking as well as post-recording transcription.

## Who is Transcribe for?

- Professionals who need meeting transcriptions
- Students transcribing lectures and study materials
- Journalists and interviewers converting recordings to text
- Content creators who prefer speaking to typing
- Anyone who wants fast, accurate voice-to-text conversion on iPhone

Transcribe's AI engine delivers accuracy that matches professional transcription services at a fraction of the cost and time. Multi-language support makes it versatile for international use, and the multiple export formats ensure your transcripts integrate into any workflow.

Real-time transcription capability makes it useful beyond post-recording use — it works as a live note-taking tool during meetings and events. It is the most practical speech-to-text solution on iOS.
