## About Photo to PDF

Photo to PDF converts your photos and images into professional PDF documents quickly and easily on iPhone. Combine multiple images into a single multi-page PDF, adjust page layout and orientation, and create documents perfect for scanning receipts, building portfolios, or sharing photo collections.

Whether you need to submit photos as a document, create a PDF portfolio, or digitize paper documents by photographing them, Photo to PDF handles the conversion instantly. Select images from your photo library, arrange them in order, and generate a clean PDF ready to share or archive.

Adjustable page layout and orientation settings give you control over how your images appear in the final document. Create professional-looking PDFs with proper formatting, then share them via email, messaging, cloud storage, or any other method your iPhone supports.

## Who is Photo to PDF for?

- Anyone who needs to convert photos or scanned documents to PDF
- Professionals creating PDF portfolios from images
- Students submitting photo-based assignments as documents
- People digitizing paper documents, receipts, and records
- iPhone users who need to combine multiple images into one PDF
