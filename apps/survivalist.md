## About Survivalist

Survivalist is your comprehensive guide to emergency preparedness and survival skills on iPhone. Whether you are preparing for natural disasters, learning wilderness survival techniques, or building self-sufficiency skills, this app provides detailed guides and checklists to help you prepare for anything.

The app covers a wide range of survival scenarios — from wilderness emergencies and natural disasters to power outages and urban survival. Each topic includes step-by-step techniques, equipment checklists, and practical knowledge that could prove critical in an emergency situation.

All content is available offline, which is essential for a survival reference — you cannot count on having internet access when you need this information most. Download the guides to your device and have a reliable survival knowledge base in your pocket at all times.

## Who is Survivalist for?

- Anyone who wants to be prepared for emergencies and disasters
- Outdoor enthusiasts and hikers who need wilderness survival knowledge
- Preppers building self-sufficiency skills and emergency kits
- Campers wanting a portable survival reference guide
- Families creating emergency preparedness plans on iPhone

## Why choose Survivalist?

Survivalist provides structured, actionable survival knowledge rather than scattered tips. The checklist-based approach helps you systematically prepare for different scenarios, ensuring you do not overlook critical items or skills.

Offline access makes it a truly reliable emergency resource. When connectivity fails — which is often the case in emergencies — your survival guide is still fully accessible on your iOS device.
