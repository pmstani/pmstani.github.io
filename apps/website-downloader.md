## About Website Downloader

Website Downloader is a Safari extension for macOS that saves complete web pages to your local disk, preserving HTML, CSS, images, and all associated assets for offline access. When you need to archive important content, read offline, or back up web pages, this extension handles it with a single click from the Safari toolbar.

Unlike simple page-saving methods that lose formatting and images, Website Downloader preserves the full structure of a web page. The downloaded files look and function just as they do online, complete with styling, images, and layout — giving you a true offline copy of the original content.

The extension is ideal for archiving content that might change or disappear, preparing reading material for offline access during travel, and creating local backups of important web resources. Quick downloads from the toolbar mean you can save any page without interrupting your browsing flow.

## Who is Website Downloader for?

- Researchers archiving web content for reference and citation
- Students saving course materials and articles for offline study
- Professionals backing up important web resources
- Travelers preparing reading material for offline access
- Anyone who wants to preserve web content locally on Mac

## Why choose Website Downloader?

Website Downloader preserves web pages completely, not just their text. HTML, CSS, and images are all saved, so your offline copies faithfully represent the original pages without broken layouts or missing assets.

The one-click Safari toolbar integration makes saving pages frictionless. You can quickly archive anything you find important while browsing, building a local library of web content that is always accessible on macOS — even without an internet connection.
