## About Mythos

Mythos helps you discover your psychological archetype through engaging quizzes rooted in mythology and Jungian psychology. Explore ancient archetypes that have shaped human storytelling for millennia, and gain surprising insights about your personality patterns, motivations, and inner self.

Carl Jung's archetype theory suggests that certain universal patterns of behavior are embedded in our collective unconscious. Mythos makes this rich psychological framework accessible through interactive quizzes that map your responses to mythological archetypes like the Hero, the Sage, the Creator, and more. Each archetype comes with detailed profiles and descriptions that illuminate how these patterns show up in your life.

Share your archetype results with friends and compare profiles to understand the different patterns that drive the people around you. Mythos turns deep psychology into an engaging, social experience that sparks meaningful conversations about personality and self-understanding.

Mythos goes deeper than typical personality quizzes by grounding its framework in established Jungian psychology and mythology. The archetypes are not arbitrary categories — they represent patterns recognized across cultures and centuries of human storytelling.

The detailed archetype profiles provide genuine insight rather than superficial labels. Combined with the social sharing and comparison features, Mythos offers an experience that is both personally meaningful and enjoyable to explore on iOS.
