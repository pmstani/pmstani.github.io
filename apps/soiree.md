## About Soirée

Soirée is the beautiful way to plan your wedding or event seating and turn it into print-ready stationery you will be proud to display. You arrange tables on a simple drag-and-drop canvas, manage your whole guest list, and export gorgeous PDFs — all on iPhone, iPad and Mac, and all with the same file wherever you work.

The floor plan is built for real rooms. You place round, banquet, head, square, oval and sweetheart tables at true-to-life sizes and seat counts, then add the dance floor, stage, DJ, bar, buffet, gift and cake tables, doors and walls. Zoom, pan, snap, duplicate and one-tap rotate make it quick to lay out a venue exactly as it will be on the day, and undo means nothing is ever a risk. Every guest lives in a proper guest list with households, plus-ones and children, RSVP status, meal choices, notes and tags — imported from Contacts or a CSV spreadsheet if you already have one. Keep-together and keep-apart rules raise live conflict and capacity warnings, so you can see at a glance who is still unseated and whether any table is over its limit.

What most seating tools cannot do is print. Soirée includes a Print Studio that turns your plan into finished stationery: a seating chart poster in A2, A3, A4, Letter or Tabloid, ordered alphabetically or by table; escort cards; tent-fold place cards; per-table number cards; and a printable alphabetical guest list. Editorial themes match the app so the output looks designed rather than generated, and everything exports as an AirPrint-ready PDF you can print at home or hand to a print shop.

Soirée is a buy-once app. There is no subscription, no account and no ads, and it works completely offline — even in a venue basement with no signal. Your guest list never leaves your device.

## Who is Soirée for?

- Couples planning their own wedding seating who want a calm, visual way to arrange tables and guests
- Wedding planners and coordinators who manage several events and need reliable, professional output
- Anyone hosting a gala, banquet, corporate dinner, reception, birthday or celebration where seating matters
- People who want to print their own seating chart, escort cards and place cards instead of paying a stationer for every revision
- Privacy-minded hosts who would rather keep their guest list on their own device than in someone else's cloud

## Why choose Soirée?

Soirée is designed around the two halves of the job that other apps split apart: planning the room, and producing the stationery that comes out of it. Because the floor plan and the Print Studio share the same data, a change to who sits where flows straight through to the poster, the escort cards and the place cards — there is no re-typing names into a separate design tool, and no risk of a card that disagrees with the chart.

It is also built to be trustworthy on the day. Soirée works fully offline, so a weak signal at the venue never leaves you locked out of your own plan, and it is a native app on iPhone, iPad and Mac rather than one cross-platform shell — you can rough out tables on your phone, do the detailed work on a Mac or iPad, and open the same file everywhere.

Finally, Soirée respects your data and your wallet. There is no subscription and no account to create, you pay once and own it, and your guest list stays on your device and is never uploaded or tracked. For a job as personal as who sits next to whom at your wedding, that combination of print-ready output, offline reliability and genuine privacy is what sets it apart.
