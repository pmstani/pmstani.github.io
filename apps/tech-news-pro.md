## About Tech News Pro

Tech News Pro delivers curated technology news from Hacker News and other top sources in a clean, distraction-free reading experience on iPhone. Cut through the noise of mainstream tech coverage and get the stories that matter most to developers, startup founders, and technology professionals.

The app curates the best stories from Hacker News, filtering out low-quality content and surfacing the discussions and articles that the tech community finds most valuable. Customizable news categories let you focus on the topics you care about, whether that is programming, startups, AI, security, or any other technology domain.

Save articles for offline reading so you can catch up during commutes or flights without an internet connection. The distraction-free reader strips away ads and clutter, presenting just the content in a format optimized for comfortable reading on your iPhone.

## Who is Tech News Pro for?

- Developers who follow Hacker News and want a better mobile reading experience
- Startup founders staying current with technology and business trends
- Technology professionals who need curated, high-quality news
- Programmers seeking community-driven tech content without the noise
- Anyone who wants a cleaner alternative to mainstream tech news apps

## Why choose Tech News Pro?

Tech News Pro respects your time by curating instead of flooding. Rather than showing everything, it surfaces the most valuable stories from the Hacker News community, saving you the effort of sifting through hundreds of submissions.

The distraction-free reader and offline support make it practical for real-world use. Whether you have five minutes during a break or an hour on a flight, Tech News Pro delivers quality tech news in a format that works on iOS.
