## About Audit++

Audit++ is a Safari extension for macOS that performs comprehensive website audits covering SEO, performance, accessibility, and security. Get actionable insights and detailed reports that help you improve any website's quality, user experience, and search engine ranking — all from within your browser.

Running a thorough website audit typically requires multiple tools and services. Audit++ consolidates the most important checks into a single extension that analyzes pages in real time. SEO factors like meta tags, heading structure, and keyword usage are evaluated alongside performance metrics, accessibility compliance, and security best practices.

The detailed reports do not just identify problems — they provide specific recommendations for fixing them. Prioritized findings help you focus on the changes that will have the biggest impact, making Audit++ a practical tool for ongoing website improvement.

## Who is Audit++ for?

- SEO professionals auditing client websites
- Web developers checking their sites for issues before launch
- Marketing teams monitoring website quality and search optimization
- Accessibility advocates ensuring websites meet compliance standards
- Website owners who want to improve their site's performance on Mac

## Why choose Audit++?

Audit++ covers four critical dimensions — SEO, performance, accessibility, and security — in a single tool. This comprehensive approach catches issues that single-purpose tools miss, giving you a complete picture of website quality.

The actionable recommendations transform audit findings into a clear improvement plan. Instead of just listing problems, Audit++ tells you how to fix them and what to prioritize, making it the most practical website audit tool for Safari on macOS.
